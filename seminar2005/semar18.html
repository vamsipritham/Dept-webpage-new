Event Title          : "Exotic R^4's and Ozsvath-Szabo invarients"

                            

Speaker              : Siddartha Gadgil

Affiliation            : ISI, Bangalore

Subject Area       : Mathematics

Date                   : March 18, 2005

Time                   : 4.15 pm

Venue                 : Lecture Hall I, Depat of Mathematics

Abstract                 : We use the invarients of Ozsvath and Szabo to construct an invarient of open 4-mainfolds. We construct a 4-mainfold x which is homeomorphic to R^4 and show using our invarients that x is not diffeomorphic to R^4. This is the first invariant for open manifolds that directly recognses exotic R^4s.
