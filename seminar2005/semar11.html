Event Title          : "On analyzing the unramified principal series of reductive groups"       

                            

Speaker              : Dr. Amritanshu Prasad

Affiliation            : Institute for Mathematical Sciences, Chennai

Subject Area       : Mathematics

Date                   : March 11, 2005

Time                   : 4.15 pm

Venue                 : Lecture Hall I, Depat of Mathematics

Abstract                 : A large class or irreducible representations of reductive group over a P-adic field can be contructed as subquotients of representations induced from a Borel subgroup. We describe some techniques for analyzing these representations.
