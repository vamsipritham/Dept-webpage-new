"Maximal and Minimul curves over Finite Fields"
Abstract  

 
 We define maximal and minimal curves over finite fields. We describe the inter-relationship that exists between maximal and minimal curves, when these curves are considered over all finite extensions of the base field.  We end by describing some explicite results we have obtained
for  certain hyperelliptic curves, which are maximal and minimal, in the context of certain questions of interest in the literature.
