Event Title1

:

“Special values of zeta functions”


 

 
Abstract  

     Many people starting with Euler have studied special values of zeta functions (such as the Riemann zeta function).  We will provide a leisurely motivation to this subject with some examples, while pointing out its connections to other parts of mathematics.
