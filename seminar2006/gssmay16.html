Event Title1

: 

Strong proximinality in C(K)(Part- II)
 
Abstract  

 We explore differentiability in C(K) space and in its dual, where K is compact Hausdorff. As a consequence, we show that strong proximinality is transitive among finite codimensional subspaces of C(K).
