Event Title

: 

Some applications of the big Chern classes
Abstract :

In this talk, we shall describe the big Chern classes, which were discovered independently by Kapranov and Nori, and indicate how their properties can be used to show the nonexistence of certain morphisms between Grassmannians over a field of characteristic 0.
