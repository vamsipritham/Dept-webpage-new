Event Title

: 

Growth rates for random graphs
Abstract : Starting with two vertices V1, V2 that are connected vertices are added with connection to existing vertices with probability proportional to their degrees.  It will be shown that as the number n of vertices grow the degree of vertices grow at rate √2.  Some generalization will also be indicated.
