Title

: 

Embedding hyperbolic Dynkin diagrams into E_{10}
Abstract :

Classical Dynkin diagrams are used in the classification of finite dimensional simple Lie algebras. I will describe other types of Dynkin diagrams -- the so called affine and hyperbolic types -- and show how the hyperbolic diagrams can all be realized as subdiagrams (in a certain precise sense) of a special diagram denoted E_{10}. This implies corresponding statements about certain Lie algebras and reflection groups.
