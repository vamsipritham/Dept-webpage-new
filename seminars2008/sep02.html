Title

: 

Automorphisms of Bounded Domains
Abstract :

The purpose of this series of lectures is to discuss the Greene-Krantz semi-continuity theorems for automorphism groups and the theorems of Bedford-Dadok and Saerens-Zame on realizing compact Lie groups as the automorphism group of bounded strongly pseudoconvex domains. The question of realizing closed subgroups of the given automorphism group by perturbation of the boundary of the domain will also be considered.
