Title

: 

Some uncertainty principles on solvable Lie groups
Abstract :

The aim of this series of lectures is to seek analogies of some known uncertainty principles, proved in the case of the real line to certain solvable Lie groups. We will speak about uncertainty principles of Hardy, Cowling ~V Price, Morgan and Beurling and present some recent results on their non ~V commutative analogues. Furthermore, we will also talk about sharpness of the decay condition in Hardy~Rs principle and give some new result in that context.
