Title

: 

"Boundary behaviour of the Kobayashi distance and horosphere
boundary in complete hyperbolic manifolds."
Abstract

: 

In this joint work with Filippo Bracci, we define the horosphere
boundary for complete hyperbolic manifolds, we present sharp estimates for
the Kobayashi distance implying that the horosphere boundary of a strongly
pseudoconvex domain is homeomorphic to its euclidean boundary and we
present some application of the construction of the horosphere boundary.
