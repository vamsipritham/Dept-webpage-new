Title

: 

"The chiral boson and function theory on the unit disc"
Abstract

: 

 Conformal field theories (CFTs) are related (in Mathematics) to algebraic geometry, infinite-dimensional Lie algebras and probability, 
 and (in Physics) to critical phenomena and string theory. From a mathematical point of view, much of the formalisation has been from 
 the point of view of algebra -- in fact using formal power series. I will give a denition of the simplest "chiral" or "holomorphic" CFT using 
 elementary function theory. If time permits, I will also explain operator product expansions.
