Title

: 

"A Google Course-builder course - Hyperbolic Spaces and Groups"
Abstract

: 

  Google has recently released open source software  called `course-builder' (at https://code.google.com/p/course-builder/) to
 make interactive online courses consisting of videos interleaved with quizzes. In this brief presentation, I will show as an example a
 course-builder course I made and describe the process of making such courses. My goal is to convince that making online courses is both
 easy and worthwhile.
