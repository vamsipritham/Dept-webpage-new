Title

"Homogeneous dynamics and number theory"
Abstract

: 

 I will discuss some instances of the interplay between dynamics on homogeneous spaces of algebraic groups and Diophantine approximation,
 with an emphasis on recent developments. The latter includes joint work with Gorodnik and Nevo, and with Einsiedler and Lytle.
