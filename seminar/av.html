Title: Grothendieck-Serre Formula and Bigraded Cohen-Macaulay Rees Algebras
Speaker: Dr. A.V. Jayanthan
Affiliation: School of Mathematics, TIFR , MUMBAI
Abstract/Brief Description: Characterization of Cohen-Macaulay property of bigraded Rees algebra of two m-primary ideals in a Cohen-Macaulay local ring of dimension two, using the Bhattacharya polynomial, its coefficients and reduction numbers. The result is obtained as an application of a generalized Grothendieck-Serre formula for the difference of Bhattacharya function and polynomial in terms of lengths of the bigraded components of the local cohomology modules.
Subject Area: Mathematics
Date: Friday, March 5, 2004
Time: 4:00pm-5:00pm
Duration: 60 minutes
Venue: Lecture Hall - I, Department of Mathematics, IISc.
