Event Title:

Fusion and Spatio-temporal Patterns in (2+1) Dimensional K-dV Type Equations

Speaker:

Dr. R.Radha

Affiliation:

Department of Physics
Govt.College for Women
Kumbakonam
Bangalore - 560 012

Abstract/Brief Description:

In this talk, we construct exponentrally localized solutions (dromions) of the (2+1) dimensional K-dV type equation and explain their unelastic colliscon. We then drive the arbitrary functions (boundaries) floating in the system in the form of a chaotic oscillator and study the onslaught of the chaotic oscillator on the coherent structure to generate new spatio-temporal patterns.
