Event Title: Parameter estimation in groundwater flow
Speaker: Prof. Volker Schulz
Affiliation: FB IV - Mathematics, University of Trier, 54286 Trier, Germany
Abstract/Brief Description: Parameter estimation in differential equation models is an important task to be performed before achieving a useful model in practical applications. In this talk, numerical methods for inverse modeling of evolution processes will be discussed. Recent results for the application field of groundwater flow will be presented.
Subject Area: Mathematics


Date: Wednesday, April 21, 2004
Time: 4:00pm
Venue: Lecture Hall I, Mathematics Department, IISC., Bangalore-12
