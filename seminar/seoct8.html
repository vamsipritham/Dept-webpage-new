Event Title:

Numerical Solution of Fredholm Integral Equation of Second Kind And Corresponding Eigenvalue Problems

Speaker:

Dr.N.Gnaneshwar

Affiliation:

Department of Mathematics, IISC

Abstract/Brief Description:

We consider numerical solution of eigenvalue problem and solution of corresponding operator equation of second kind associated with compact operator T defined on a complex Banach Space. Using a new suiperconvergent approximating operator we investigate two iterative refinement schemes for eigenvalue problem and obtain order of convergence. We show that the new method performs better than other projection methods. And we develop an iterative refinement scheme for operator equation of second kind
