Event Title:

Modular and Integral Representations of GL_n

Speaker:

Prof. Upendra Kulkarni

Affiliation:

Truman State University

Abstract/Brief Description:

Representation theory of split reductive algebraic groups in positive characteristic is somewhat parallel to the BGG category of representations of complex semisimple Lie algebras. But in the former situation the character formulas for simple modules are still unknown. I will sketch the main points in our current state of  knowledge. I will then indicate how extensions between integral weyl modules enter the picture, for example in a new interpretation of Jantzen’s sum formula and in proving Andersen’s tilting sum formula. The full significance of these Ext groups remains mysterious. Specializing to GL_n
