Title

: 

Elimination for PDE
Abstract :

In this talk I introduce a notion of "geometric completeness" for spaces
of distributions, modelled after the notion of a complete variety in
algebraic geometry. It is related to the followng "elimination problem"
for systems of PDE: consider the set of homogeneous solutions of a system
of PDE in some space of distributions. When is the projection of this set
onto some of its co-ordinates also the set of homogeneous solutions of a
system of PDE?
