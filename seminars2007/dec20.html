Title

 
Holomorphic extension of CR functions from hypersurfaces with singularities

Abstract

: 

We show that Trepreau's theorem (minimality of a hypersurface at a point implies one sided extension of all CR functions) does not hold if
the hypersurface is allowed to have singularities. We formulate a geometric condition called two sided support which is the obstruction
to such extension.
