Title

: 

Regularity of the Solutions and the Free Boundary in the Composite Membrane Problem
Abstract : The composite membrane question is to build a body of prescribed shape, and ofprescribed mass, with materials of varying density so as to minimize the first Dirichleteigenvalue. It naturally leads to a free boundary problem. The regularity of the freeboundary is connected with monotonicity formulae, that make their appearance in severalareas of Geometry: minimal surfaces, Poincare conjecture and harmonic maps to name afew. We will present such a regularity theory. This is joint work
with Carlos Kenig.
