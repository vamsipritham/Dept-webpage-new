Title

: 

Congruent numbers
Abstract : We discuss the ancient problem of charactersing rational numbers
which can be the area of a right triangle with rational sides. While this
problem is still unsolved, important progress has been made recently. We
shall describe these advances in a language accessible to a wide
mathematical audience.
